Exclusive CSD Program for Armed Forces

 

Nissan proudly salutes the valour and dedication of the Army, Navy, and Air Force personnel through its CSD program. As a token of our gratitude, eligible members can enjoy exclusive discounts on approved Nissan models along with concessional GST rates. This initiative ensures a seamless and rewarding car ownership experience, tailored for our nation’s heroes.
