Sustainable Future


Nissan’s global environmental philosophy and principles extend beyond the present. From recycling parts to increasing the use of second life EV batteries, we are focused on reducing our environmental impact and resource consumption. The aim is to contribute to a cleaner, safer and more sustainable future.

Mitigating Environmental Footprint

Nissan is working to achieve carbon neutrality through initiatives based on new technologies, renewable energy sources (battery ecosystem) and reusable packaging materials.

Renewable Solutions on products

Nissan aims to provide eco-friendly solutions considering without compromising quality and performance. Our efforts involve expanding remanufacturing parts (engine, transmission, suspension and more), EV batteries and the use of recycled accessories such as velour mats.
