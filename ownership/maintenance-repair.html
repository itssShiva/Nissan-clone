AIR CONDITIONING SERVICE

Air quality inside your Nissan is very important for a safe journey. Considering the dusty conditions in India, your car’s Air Conditioning function can get impaired with dust, pollen and insects.

Our experts will examine the operating controls, inspect all system components including the compressor, cooler, and condenser and measure the outlet air temperature.

You should get your car’s AC serviced for the following reasons:

  • Remove any pollen, dust, microorganisms and soot which may lead to breathing disorders.
  • Improve visibility due to the windscreen being less susceptible to misting over
  • Reduce any chances of irritation of the eyes and the respiratory tract
  • Ensure a better concentration, even on longer journeys.

Get your Air Conditioning checked just before the Summers or through one of the regular ‘Air  Conditioning / Cooling Campaigns’ at our Nissan Service Centres where you can avail wonderful discounts and offers.

FAQ's

1.    Can I service My vehicle at local service centre? Do I need to service in at authorised dealer?

You should service at authorised dealer only. Following your Nissan recommended maintenance schedule is vital to your vehicle’s performance. And you can trust our well-trained technicians to make sure your maintenance is done right. Enjoy our best-in-class services to have flawless driving experience. This is very much necessary for honouring warranty and extended warranty.

2.    What will be the Servicing cost of my vehicle?

Please check the cost of services at our service recommendation at click here or contact your nearest Authorised dealer.

3.    Can I ask the dealership for pick up and drop facility for car servicing?

Yes. Please ask your nearest dealership for pick up and drop facility, it is available at nominal price.

4.    When should I service my Nissan vehicle?

Every model has a recommended service interval for maintenance. Please refer to owner's manual for the recommended service interval or contact your nearest authorised dealer.

5.    I have a service concern with my vehicle, what should I do?

If you have a concern with your vehicle, you should contact nearest Authorise Dealer for any further assistance. You can also contact at 1800 209 3456 for your concern.

6.    I am not getting support from my dealership, where should I escalate?

Nissan is committed for 100% customer satisfaction. Should a problem arise from the servicing or maintenance of your vehicle, you should discuss the issue with your Dealer’s Service Manager. If the issue is still not resolved, please contact directly on 1800 209 3456.

7.    What is Nissan Connect App?

Nissan Connect is Nissan Connected car application for all Nissan customers, fuelled by the passion to make ownership more convenient for every customer. To download search "NissanConnect" in Google Play or Apple App Store.

8.    What are some of the things which I can do from Nissan Connect App?

It has many features such as- Manage service booking, service tracking & history, automated Roadside assistance, vehicle details and many more.

9.    What is Nissan Roadside Assistance?

Whether you’ve locked your keys in your car, got a flat tyre, flat battery, run out of fuel, or have just simply broken down, our friendly 24/7 Roadside team will have you safely back on the road in no time. Please contact directly on 1800 209 3456. To know more - Click here

10.    How can I avail RSA?

Nissan ensure peace of mind for all our customers. RSA services can be availed free for 2 Yrs/ 3 Yrs*** from the date of vehicle purchase. Post that you can extend the services by paying nominal charges. Please contact your nearest Authorised dealership for more information.
