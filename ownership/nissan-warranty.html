FAQ's

1.   What is the standard coverage of the New Vehicle Warranty?

For Nissan Brand: 1) Nissan Magnite- 2yrs/ 50k km,  2) New Nissan Gravite, Magnite & Xtrail- 3yrs/ 1Lac Kms (whichever is earlier).

2.   I have a concern with my vehicle and would like to know whether it will be covered under warranty.

Unfortunately, we are unable to confirm whether your concern will be covered under warranty, as the vehicle would need to be inspected by an Authorised Dealership. If the failure is determined to be as a result of a defect in factory materials or workmanship, this will be covered under the terms of the Express New Vehicle Warranty.

3.   Can I modify my car and if so, what will happen to my warranty?

If genuine parts are fitted, warranty will be applicable. In case of non-genuine parts/accessories, warranty will be termed void for any failures related to parts installed.

4.   Are Nissan spare parts and accessories covered under Warranty?

Yes, spare parts and accessories are covered for a period of 1 year/20K km (whichever is earlier) from date of sale of part/accessory, subjected to purchase and fitment at Nissan Authorised Dealer only.

5.   How do I find out what items are covered under warranty?

You can refer WIMB (warranty information and maintenance booklet) provided with the new vehicle Alternatively, nearest dealer can be visited for getting this information.

6.   I can't find my Customer Assistance, Warranty and Service Guide - what should I do?

Contact your nearest Dealer's Service Department who can order a replacement Customer Assistance, Warranty and Service Guide on your behalf.

7.   I need an Owner’s Manual or WIMB (warranty information and maintenance booklet) of my car. Can you help?

Your local Dealer’s Parts department will be able to assist you with purchasing a replacement owner's manual. Please visit the Dealer Locator here for details of your nearest Dealership. You can also download the soft copy of the owner’s manual at.

8.   I recently sold my vehicle; can I transfer the Extended Warranty to the new owner?

Yes, the extended warranty travels with the car and can be transferred.

9.   I would like some information about the Extended Warranties.

To know more about Extended Warranty - Download Brochure

10.   If I fit aftermarket parts to my vehicle, will this affect my warranty?

We strongly recommend that you fit Genuine parts and accessories to your vehicle. All Genuine parts and accessories come with a 12 month or 20,000km warranty (whichever comes first). New Vehicle Warranty will not cover repairs to your vehicle should the problem be attributed to the installation of a non-Genuine component. For further clarification, please refer to Warranty information & maintenance booklet available with the vehicle where details related to warranty coverage.

11.   If the service is delayed than recommended time period, will this impact my warranty?

Regular scheduled servicing is vital to maintain the performance of your car. You should always follow the recommended service intervals as outlined in your vehicle's Customer Assistance, Warranty and Service Guide, including any additional service requirements that your vehicle may need. Your warranty does not cover damage caused by inadequate maintenance.

12.   In order to have warranty work carried out, do I need to return to an Authorised Dealer?

Yes, Only Authorised dealer.

13.   Is the New Vehicle Warranty transferable with the vehicle?

Yes, it travels with car.

14.   My vehicle has missed a scheduled service. Will this be a problem in relation to my Warranty?

Failure to service your vehicle in accordance with our recommendations could affect your warranty. For further clarification, please refer warranty information & maintenance booklet.

15.   What are the costs not covered by the New Vehicle Warranty?

Costs like maintenance services, cost of replacing normal wear and tear parts etc. are generally not covered under warranty. For detailed information please refer Warranty information & maintenance booklet.

16.   Can I service My vehicle at local service centre? Do I need to service it at authorised dealer?

You should service at authorised dealer only. Following your Nissan recommended maintenance schedule is vital to your vehicle’s performance. And you can trust our well-trained technicians to make sure your maintenance is done right. Enjoy our best-in-class services to have flawless driving experience. This is very much necessary for honouring warranty and extended warranty.
