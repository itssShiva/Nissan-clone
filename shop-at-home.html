FAQ's

1. What is Nissan@Home?

Nissan Nissan@home is an end-to-end digital enabled customer service, right from the convenience of your home.

2. Can I test drive a Nissan before buying it online?

Of course. You can call 1800-209-3456 to book a test drive or visit the Nissan showroom.

3. How does the remote buying process work?

The remote buying process makes life easier by saving you the effort of going to the dealership. After you submit your order, you’ll receive a confirmation email. The nearest dealer will then email you the vehicle purchase agreement and confirm you’ve reviewed and understood the document. Then, you print, sign, and return the agreement with your ID. Your vehicle will be delivered to your doorstep, while adhering to social distancing measures, and a representative will safely verify your identity.

4. What other options do i have to explore the Nissan car range?

With us, you can find plenty of options to explore our car range. Like, going to showroom and taking a look at your chosen Nissan car. But if you still want to have a look at it from the comfort of your home, tell us a time that works best for you and we’ll connect with you for a video showcase. It’s that easy.

5. Can I check how much my Nissan Service costs?

Yes. Our website offers Online Service Booking with Price Calculator - An online estimation tool where you have to feed in the details of your car and the service it requires and voila! It calculates and gives you an overview of the exact price of the car service.

6. How can I find the best deals on all Nissan cars?

Month-on-month, we put out the best deals on all Nissan cars. What makes it better is that we also offer a variety of financial options with which you can buy your Nissan with the most lucrative offers.

7. Can I apply for car loan online?

Of course, you can avail a car loan from Nissan Finance to purchase a Nissan vehicle in just 3 steps. 1. Select your vehicle and finance options – Choose your Nissan that you wish to buy. Once you decide on which car model to purchase, you can choose a finance option that suits your requirements. 2. Select protection plans – The next step is to select the add-on benefits offered by Nissan Finance. This includes motor insurance, payment protection insurance, and an extended warranty. 3. Get in touch with Nissan – You can now contact Nissan Finance and submit your car loan application form. To get in touch with Nissan Finance, you can call the toll-free number 1-800-209-3459 – Option 5 between 9:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. Monday to Friday and 9:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. on Saturday. You can also send an email to csupport.nissanfinance@nrfsi.com to get in touch with the representatives of Nissan Finance.

8. Can I configure a Nissan online with accessories and after sales value-added services?

Definitely, yes. In the Build & Price segment on our website, you can select the vehicle and the variant you want. Add interior and exterior accessories, and value-added services and proceed to book it online.
