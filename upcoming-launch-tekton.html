The All-New Nissan Tekton, inspired by the Greek word for craftsman, blends innovation, strength, and precision. The name signifies a powerful, premium C-SUV that embodies engineering excellence, performance, and a distinctive design identity.

Unveiled by Nissan’s Global Head of Design, Alfonso Albaisa, the Tekton marks a new chapter in Nissan’s SUV legacy and the beginning of product offensive in India. with a commanding stance, striking rear lightbar, and authentic SUV DNA. Made in India for global markets, the Tekton redefines modern SUV driving with boldness, confidence, and performance.
