    Overview

    ONE CAR.<br>ONE WORLD.

    The made-in-India SUV, new Nissan Magnite is set to be exported to 65+ countries around the world. And the same car that the world will drive is the one you are going to drive in India, bringing alive Nissan’s philosophy of One Car, One World.

    Configure Magnite

    BOLD INSIDE OUT

    True boldness comes from inside. The new Nissan Magnite is packed with bold features and style, inside and outside. Come explore the car that makes a bold statement like no other.

    SWITCH TO GREENER DRIVES

    <br>The New Nissan Magnite is now available with a Government Approved retro-fitment CNG kit, delivering a cleaner and more economical driving experience. Available as a retrofit, the kit comes with a 3-year/1 lakh km warranty*, giving you peace of mind on every journey.
